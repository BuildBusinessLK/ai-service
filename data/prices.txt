== COCONUT PRODUCT MARKET PRICES - SEPTEMBER 2025 ==

Price data is available on a daily basis for September 2025.
Prices are in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR).

== POONAC PRICES - SEPTEMBER 2025 ==

Poonac is a coconut by-product used mainly as animal feed.
In September 2025, poonac prices ranged between LKR 80,000 and LKR 110,000 per unit.
On September 1 to 4, poonac prices ranged from LKR 85,000 to LKR 110,000.
From September 8 to 12, poonac prices stabilized at LKR 100,000 to LKR 105,000.
On September 17, prices dipped to a range of LKR 85,000 to LKR 100,000.
On September 18, prices reached their lowest point in the month at LKR 80,000 to LKR 100,000.
On September 19, prices recovered to LKR 85,000 to LKR 100,000.
From September 22 onwards, poonac prices stabilized at LKR 90,000 to LKR 105,000.
From September 23 to 30, prices settled at LKR 100,000 to LKR 105,000.
Overall, poonac prices were relatively stable in September 2025 with a brief dip mid-month before recovering.
The minimum poonac price recorded in September 2025 was LKR 80,000.
The maximum poonac price recorded in September 2025 was LKR 110,000.

== FRESH COCONUT PRICES - SEPTEMBER 2025 ==

Fresh coconut prices in September 2025 are recorded in four categories: large wholesale, large retail, small wholesale, and small retail.
Prices are per nut in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR).

Large Coconut Wholesale Prices:
On September 1 and 2, large coconut wholesale prices ranged from LKR 170 to LKR 180.
From September 3 onwards, large coconut wholesale prices increased to LKR 180 to LKR 190, where they remained stable for the rest of the month.

Large Coconut Retail Prices:
On September 1 and 2, large coconut retail prices ranged from LKR 190 to LKR 210.
From September 3 to 22, large coconut retail prices were LKR 190 to LKR 220.
From September 23 to 30, large coconut retail prices increased slightly to LKR 190 to LKR 230.

Small Coconut Wholesale Prices:
On September 1 and 2, small coconut wholesale prices ranged from LKR 150 to LKR 160.
From September 3 onwards, small coconut wholesale prices were LKR 150 to LKR 165.
From September 23, small coconut wholesale prices increased to LKR 150 to LKR 170.

Small Coconut Retail Prices:
Throughout September 2025, small coconut retail prices remained stable at LKR 170 to LKR 190.
Fresh coconut prices were broadly stable throughout September 2025 with a slight increase in retail prices in the final week.
The large wholesale price difference between the start and end of September was approximately LKR 10 per nut.
