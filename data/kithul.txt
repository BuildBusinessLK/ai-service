== KITHUL AND JAGGERY EXPORT OVERVIEW ==

Sri Lanka exports kithul and jaggery products under HS code category 17029000 series.
Export data is available from year 2000 to 2025 (January to October) with quantity in kilograms and value in US dollars.
The kithul sector includes kithul jaggery, kithul treacle, sakkara (sugar cane jaggery), and other jaggery varieties.

== KITHUL JAGGERY EXPORTS (HS Code H.17029030) ==

Kithul jaggery is one of the primary kithul-based export products from Sri Lanka.
In 2000, kithul jaggery exports were 911 kg valued at USD 3,327.
In 2001, exports grew to 16,414 kg valued at USD 37,335.
In 2002, exports reached 34,880 kg valued at USD 111,011.
In 2003, exports were 61,106 kg valued at USD 183,225.
In 2004, exports were 74,396 kg valued at USD 228,360.
In 2005, exports were 70,460 kg valued at USD 241,037.
In 2006, exports were 68,250 kg valued at USD 245,170.
In 2007, exports grew to 101,118 kg valued at USD 343,182.
In 2008, exports reached 112,462 kg valued at USD 417,094.
In 2009, exports were 93,162 kg valued at USD 385,195.
In 2010, exports dropped to 60,221 kg valued at USD 217,547.
In 2011, exports recovered to 109,024 kg valued at USD 382,695.
In 2012, exports reached 150,219 kg valued at USD 533,081.
In 2013, exports were 68,323 kg valued at USD 265,114.
In 2014, exports grew to 86,816 kg valued at USD 349,786.
In 2015, exports were 85,711 kg valued at USD 412,536.
In 2016, exports grew to 110,467 kg valued at USD 477,674.
Kithul jaggery exports showed a strong long-term growth trend from 2000 to 2016.
The per kilogram export price of kithul jaggery increased significantly over the years, reflecting growing international demand.
