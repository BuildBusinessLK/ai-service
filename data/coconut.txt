== COCONUT INDUSTRY OVERVIEW (2024) ==

Sri Lanka has a total of 1,095,982 acres under coconut cultivation as of 2024.
Of this, 178,675 acres belong to the estate sector and 917,307 acres are smallholdings.
Total nut production in 2024 was 2,792.03 million nuts.
Household fresh nut consumption accounted for 1,414.01 million nuts.
The industry used 1,378.02 million nuts for processing and manufacturing.
Coconut products contributed 0.7% to Sri Lanka's GDP in 2024.
Coconut product exports were valued at Rs. 257,921 million (USD 855 million) in 2024.
Coconut products accounted for 6.7% of total Sri Lankan exports in 2024.
Total Sri Lankan exports in 2024 were Rs. 3,857,195 million (USD 12,772 million).

== COCONUT EXPORT STATISTICS (2000 TO 2025) ==

Sri Lanka has been exporting various coconut products continuously from 2000 to 2025 (January to October).
Export data is available for each year with quantity in kilograms and value in US dollars.
Products tracked include coco peat and fiber pith moulded products (code S.030205) and liquid coconut milk (code S.030107) among others.
